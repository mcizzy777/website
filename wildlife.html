Featured Animals

Lion

The lion is one of Africa's most powerful predators and lives in groups called prides.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land mammals. They use their trunks for breathing, drinking, and communication.

Giant Panda

Pandas spend most of their day eating bamboo and are symbols of wildlife conservation.

Eagle

Eagles have exceptional eyesight, allowing them to spot prey from great distances.

Sea Turtle

Sea turtles migrate thousands of kilometers and return to the beaches where they were born.

Tiger

Every tiger has unique stripes, just like human fingerprints.
