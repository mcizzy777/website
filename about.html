About National Geographic

Inspiring the world through exploration, science, photography, and conservation.

Our Story

National Geographic has inspired millions of people to explore and appreciate the natural world through powerful photography, scientific discoveries, and unforgettable storytelling.

From the deepest oceans to the highest mountains, every expedition helps us understand and protect our amazing planet.

Our Mission

Explore

Encourage curiosity through scientific exploration and discovery.

Educate

Share knowledge that inspires people to learn about Earth.

Protect

Support conservation efforts that help preserve wildlife and ecosystems.
